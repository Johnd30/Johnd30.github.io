Education

Iowa State University

Computer Science

Currently a senior at Iowa State University, graduating in December of 2017.

Research has been a big focus while at Iowa State University. I was an Undergraduate Research Assistant since freshman year in the Virtual Reality Application Center. Our team's focus was on collaboration within the virtual reality environment, and understanding how virtual reality can apply to the entire manufacturing process. As a research assistant I was able to guide my own research. I developed an android application increasing the collaboration of users in the same environment.




Maple Grove Senior High

Graduate

Graduated from Maple Grove Senior High in 2014.

While in school I was a student athlete; lettering in Varsity Hockey for 3 years. In 2012 we participated in the State High School Hockey Tournament.

I also enjoyed volunteering when I could. As students, we organized a service trip to Jamaica to give a hand at the local school and orphanage. I utilized my skills as a programmer to build a website that advertised our trip for fundraising, organized our plans, and informed our families back home of our travels. Additionally, I was part of our local Student Leadership group to help acclimate incoming students to the school culture.
